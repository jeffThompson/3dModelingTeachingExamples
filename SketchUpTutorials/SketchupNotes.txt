Download STL exporter plugin:
http://brettbeauregard.com/blog/2011/06/sketchup-to-stl-plugin-with-no-dialogs

To install a plugin:
1. Download (written in Ruby, fyi)
2. Find plugins folder: Window > Ruby Console
3. Type in: Sketchup.find_support_file("plugins")

There it is!  Just drag the plugin there and you're set!
http://sketchuptips.blogspot.com/2008/03/how-to-download-and-install-plugins.html
